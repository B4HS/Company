Vsober – Virtual Reality for Cannabis Addiction

A revolutionary VR experience designed to help individuals overcome cannabis dependency through immersive therapeutic simulations.

🌿 The Challenge

Millions struggle with cannabis misuse, but traditional interventions often lack engagement, personal relevance, or immersive exposure therapy tools.

🧠 The Science

Using principles from cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), exposure therapy, and habit reversal, Vsober simulates real-life triggers and reinforces coping strategies in a safe, virtual environment.

🕶️ Key Features

  • 🌆 Trigger exposure in simulated environments
  • 🧘 Guided mindfulness and craving management
  • 📈 Progress tracking and personalized pathways
  • 🤝 Optional therapist-linked sessions

👥 Who It's For

Individuals seeking a novel, evidence-informed approach to manage cannabis habits. Ideal for early-stage behavior change or relapse prevention.
